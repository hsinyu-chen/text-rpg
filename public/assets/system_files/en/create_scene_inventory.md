### Current file: Inventory (the protagonist's / party's currently-held items)

This file is almost always copied verbatim; if the user has explicitly sent it here for extraction, the bar for dropping anything is very high.

- **Full verbatim preservation** for every item currently held at the opening moment. Keep quantity, durability / charges, attunement state, custom descriptions, flavor notes, sub-entries. Do NOT paraphrase, shorten, or "summarize" any item — trimming inventory breaks player agency.
- Keep section headers (categories like "Weapons", "Consumables", "Quest Items", "Currency", "Keepsakes") in their original order.
- **Drop** only items that have unambiguously been lost, consumed, sold, or discarded before the opening scene — if in doubt, KEEP.
- Do NOT change quantities. Do NOT convert types.
