### Current file: Story Outline / Plot Summary

Produce a **concise bulleted summary of the ENTIRE plot leading up to the opening scene** — NOT just the portions that name the scene's characters or location. The new session needs continuity; a reader must be able to understand *how the world got here*.

Preferred structure:

1. A short introductory paragraph setting the framing.
2. A bulleted timeline / plot-progression list, grouped by Act / chapter / phase where applicable, covering:
   - Major events and turning points.
   - Character arcs and relationship shifts.
   - Unresolved threads and open quests that remain live at the opening.
   - Any guidance / triggered-event blocks from the original that are still relevant (keep their intent and structure; you may condense long triggers to 1–2 lines).
3. Keep each bullet short (1–2 sentences). Drop minutiae, but **NEVER cut plot causality** — if A caused B, the recap must still make that clear.

Do NOT include the opening scene narrative itself — the system writes it elsewhere.
