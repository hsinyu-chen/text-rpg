# Major Factions

## Kingdom (The Kingdom)
* **Nature**: Guardian of humanity, legitimate ruling power.
* **Current Status**: 
    In a state of war preparation, guarding against the invasion of the Demon Army. The King is old, and the succession issue is turbulent.

## Demon Army (Demon Army)
* **Nature**: Evil force threatening the world.
* **Current Status**: 
    The Demon Lord has just awakened, monster activities are becoming frequent, vanguard troops have appeared at the border.

## Adventurer's Guild (Adventurer's Guild)
* **Nature**: Neutral armed force intermediary organization.
* **Current Status**: 
    Actively recruiting newcomers, posting a large number of subjugation and gathering quests.

# Core World View

## Demon Lord Resurrection
* **Source**: Ancient Prophecy.
* **Essence**: Resurrects every thousand years, bringing destruction.
* **Current Status**: Confirmed that the Demon Lord "Zaror" has awakened in the Northern Demon Realm.

# Key Items

## Sword of the Brave
* **Description**: The legendary holy sword, the only one capable of killing the Demon Lord.
* **Components**:
    * **Blade**: Currently missing, said to be stuck in a stone somewhere.
    * **Scabbard**: Kept by the Fairy Queen.
