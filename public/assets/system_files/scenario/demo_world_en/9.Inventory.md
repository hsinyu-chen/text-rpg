# Inventory

> **Scope**: This file records ONLY items **owned by the protagonist** that are **carried (on-person)** — pocket, hand, backpack, portable spaces, etc. (whether something is "carried" is judged by you based on setting), **including the protagonist's pocket cash and any small valuables they keep on them**. **Items deposited at bases, inns, or third-party safekeeping** (non-carried, including cash held in a base vault) belong in `4.Assets.md` under the corresponding base layout. **NPCs' and companions' personal items** belong in `3.Character_Status.md` under that character's `### Known Significant Possessions` subsection.

## Save Format
```
## 【[Storage Location]】[Container Name]
* **Current Total Mass**: Approx. [Value] KG

### [Category Title, e.g., Ingredients, Equipment]

* [Item Name] / [Quantity]
* [Item Name] / [Quantity]
    - [Remarks]
```
