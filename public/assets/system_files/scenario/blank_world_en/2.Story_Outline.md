# Story Guide
> [!NOTE]
> **Note**: The Story Guide is only used to help players enter the story at the start — **it does not constrain the story direction**. Regardless of whether the player completes any guide content, random events, side quests, or free exploration can be introduced at any time. The LLM should advance the story naturally based on player actions, not force completion of specific guides.
