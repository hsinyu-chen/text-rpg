Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to TextRPG. We respect your privacy and are committed to transparency about how our application works. This privacy policy explains how data is handled when you use our application.

Key Point: TextRPG is a client-side application. We do not operate any servers that collect, store, or process your personal data.

No Data Collection

TextRPG is a 100% client-side application that runs entirely in your browser or on your desktop. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers because we do not have any servers.

All your data remains on your device or in your own third-party accounts that you choose to connect.

Web Hosting

When you access TextRPG through a web browser, the hosting server may collect standard web server logs, which typically include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Access timestamps
  • Pages requested

This is standard for any website and is used solely for security and debugging purposes. No game data, personal information, or user content is ever sent to our servers.

Local Data Storage

The application stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game save files: Your story progress and character data
  • Application settings: Your preferences and configurations
  • API keys: Your own API credentials (stored only on your device)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to use cloud sync features.

Google Drive Integration

If you choose to enable cloud sync, TextRPG uses Google Drive's "appDataFolder" - a private, hidden folder accessible only by this application. When you use this feature:

  • Your game data is stored in your own Google Drive account
  • We cannot access your Google Drive or your stored data
  • The connection is made directly between your browser/app and Google's servers
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings

Third-Party Services

TextRPG connects directly to the following third-party services from your device:

  • Google OAuth: For optional cloud sync authentication
  • Google Drive API: For optional cloud storage of game data
  • Gemini API: For AI-powered game content (using your own API key)

These connections are made directly from your device to the respective services. We do not act as an intermediary and have no visibility into these communications.

Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete local data by clearing browser storage or uninstalling the desktop app
  • Delete cloud data directly from your Google Drive
  • Revoke Google access through your Google Account security settings
  • The application works fully offline without any third-party connections

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
